Catch Mitao

A Pygame which can be played in the same way as Whac-A-Mole. The idea is from a WeChat cartoon cat called Mitao.

Like in a Whac-A-Mole game, the more cats you catch, the higher you score.

Background music and soundeffects were selected from free sound website.

It also has the function of managing bookmarks by tags or folders and amending any piece of the bookmark information when you feel necessary.
